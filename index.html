    OUR SERVICES


    Long Distence

    Long distance cargo is the transportation of goods over a long distance, typically between different countries or continents. Long distance cargo is an essential part of the global economy, as it allows businesses to move goods efficiently and cost-effectively.


    High Value

    High-value cargo is any cargo that has a high monetary value, such as jewelry, electronics, or pharmaceuticals. It is often considered to be any cargo that has a declared value of $10,000 or more.


    Over Night Fieght

    Overnight cargo is a type of shipping that guarantees delivery of a shipment the next day. This type of shipping is typically used for time-sensitive or critical shipments.


    Hazmat

    Hazardous materials that can pose a risk to people, property, or the environment if not properly stored, packaged, and transported. Hazmats can be solids, liquids, or gases, and they are classified into nine hazard classes based on their properties.


    Fragile Cargo

    Cargo that can break easily if exposed to falls, shocks, and rough handling during transport. It typically includes items made of glass, porcelain, or other delicate materials.


    Oversized and Over Wieght

    It takes more than a standard semi-trailer to deliver these loads! When a cargo gets heavy, it's time for more axles to satisfy the DOT and make it safely across that bridge! Introducing complex multi-joint trailers with jeeps and boosters spreading the weight of the cargo.
